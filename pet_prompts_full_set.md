- **Anatomy lock:** every pet, at every rarity and every stage, keeps the same goat-skull/horns/round-glow-eyes/floppy-ears/wings/ringed-tail identity established in the approved reference image — cute-macabre, never horror (no hollow black voids, no bared snarls, no gore/decay texture).

- **Rarity sets environment + base ceiling.** Reuses the original 5-tier environment rotation (cobblestone city -> jungle marsh -> sunset battlefield -> ice cave -> ghost-vortex mountain). Per your note, higher-rarity pets look more refined even at their Base stage (glossier coat at rare, up to faintly luminous markings at mythic), while common stays intentionally plain.
- **Evolution = growth + gear.** Base is a small, big-headed hatchling with no equipment. Each stage grows the body toward a full adult and adds gear (cord -> collar+charm -> collar+wrap -> fully equipped ornate set), with aura intensity climbing toward that pet's rarity ceiling by Evolution 4.
- **Signature accent** gives each of the 25 pets one small distinguishing detail loosely tied to its name/flavor (e.g. Chimera's tiny second tail-head, Phoenix Spirit's flame-gold wingtips), without breaking the shared anatomy.
